Brooks

Brooks Oswald has utilized various art mediums such as drawing, digital painting, photography, and filming throughout his entire life.
However, he didn't begin airbrushing until his 40th birthday when his wife bought him his first airbrush. From then on, he was hooked.
He learned various techniques by watching hours of YouTube videos, reading through forums and websites, and bouncing ideas off local model builders. He has since won mutiple awards at OmaCon IPMS and Hobby Town's yearly competitions. He enjoys teaching others the methods he has learned and has helped others win numerous awards as well. He believes that everyone can excel at art if they are willing to practice, fail, pay attention, and not give up.
